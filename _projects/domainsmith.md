# Domainsmith

**Domainsmith was an NLP powered platform that helped brand designers and startup founders discover high quality, available domain names.** Finding a good domain typically takes weeks of manual searching. Domainsmith reduced that to under an hour.

I started with a concierge MVP, running AI leveraged domain name workshops with real customers to validate pricing and product market fit before writing any code. I ran 100+ feedback sessions to refine positioning, shape messaging, and optimise the search experience. I built the full product with a technical co founder using Python, Nuxt.js, and Deta.sh.

Go to market started with workshops run through a local chamber of commerce, helping new entrepreneurs find domain names as part of their early setup. From there I built and tested a conversion focused landing page and onboarding flow to communicate the value of NLP powered domain search.

Development was paused after relocating to Cornwall. The region lacked proximity to our target users, and customer acquisition costs were too high to sustain growth remotely.
