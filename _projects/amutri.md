# Amutri

**Amutri converts complex CAD models into interactive 3D presentations that can be shared and understood across entire organisations.** In the SMR nuclear sector, where the technology is still in development, these presentations make the work tangible for investment pitches, early sales conversations, and regulatory alignment.

I took this product from concept to its first enterprise revenue. Over a 6 month pilot I delivered 13 core features including Secure Sharing and Guided Tours, which let non technical stakeholders turn engineering files into shareable presentations. I owned product, design, and data across the business, directed teams across frontend, backend, Unreal Engine, and 3D content creation, and handled everything from roadmap to UI to product analytics.

Beyond product I led onboarding sessions with enterprise customers, created go to market assets including demo videos and investor decks, and built internal tooling for AWS log monitoring and backend reliability.
